Proposal for Coca Cola Zero Commericial

After submitting my idea for a Coke Zero Commercial with my client's desired points as guidelines for the direction of the video, I designed a commercial presentation using Google Slides and edited it using Adobe Premiere Pro. I used an Iphone to capture the footage with, attached with a microphone for audio recording.

Project Requirements:
  • The project required that the length be o longer than 30 seconds
  • Must aim for a health conscious crowd.
  • The commercial must spcify no calories, no added sugar, and same great taste as regular Coka Cola.
