LIM College's 75th Annual Fashion Show

After submitting ideas and leading discussions regarding the best direction for the logo with my client's design guidelines in mind, I designed a custom logo using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.

Then using audio and video footage from stock sites, I went on to create a video logo intro using Adobe After Effects. Then I finalized the event's digital and print invitations using Adobe InDesign to be sent out to guests in time for the show.

Project Requirements:
  • The project required A logo with a diamond and the number 75 for the 75th anniversarry of the fashion show.
  • The name of the instititution must be clearly visible on the logo.
  • The Invitations must be both Horizontal and Verical.
  • The Logo Intro must have repeating scrolling text at the bottom. Clear and visible.
  • All files must be optimized for Web and HD video production.
