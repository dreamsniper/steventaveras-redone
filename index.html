A Little About Me

I'm a New York City artist looking to make a mark in the world using my creativity. My skills include Motion Graphics, Logo Design, Graphic Design, Responsive Web Design, Wireframing, Prototyping, and Character Design.

As an alumnus of Borough Of Manhattan Community College, I received my Associate's in Multimedia Programming and am part of the international honor society: Phi Theta Kappa. I am currently attending the New York College of Technology and am majoring in Emerging Media and Game Design.

When I'm not coding, I'm possibly writing short stories, reading science fiction/fantasy novels, volunteering to clean NYC's public parks, or hanging out with friends and family. I'm an illustrator and comic book artist with a powerful drive to be creative and build something great with the right team.

Local Weather App
A Project for FreeCodeCamp

This was a project for www.freecodecamp.org This project was an excercise in using API's and Geolaction services in order to pinpoint your location and use it to populate the DOM with information about your local weather.

Project Requirements:

User Story 1: I can see the weather in my current location.

User Story 2: I can see a different icon or background image (e.g. snowy mountain, hot desert) depending on the weather.

User Story 3: I can push a button to toggle between Fahrenheit and Celsius.

Tools Used:

  • HTML/CSS/SASS
  • JavaScript/jQuery
  • Bootstraps Framework
  • OpenWeatherMap API
  • Google Geolocation Services
