LIM College's Expose Fashion Show

Expose The Pose

Preliminary Designs

Just as a note this project was never completed due to COVID19 having cancelled so many events so i will show only some fun concept samples. I was tasked with showing some fresh ideas regarding thier new fashion show. The idea was "Pose" like the show critically acclaimed hit show on FX with the same name. The idea was to use Magentas and Neon Pinks. Feathers and or just letters for the title with a flashy reveal.

After sending my first two ideas they stopped me and asked me to go with the neon lettters. So I went ahead and started to create a logo reveal.

I had started to get all the elements for the logo reveal together before I was asked to get to create an animated electronic invite.

These are samples of the final logo that was approved by the school. Clean, simple and elegant with all of the elements they asked for. The Eventbrite flyers were designe d for both a vertical and horizotal option.
